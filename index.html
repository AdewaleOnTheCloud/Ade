Featured Projects

BTS Motors: Streamlining Sales & Support with Salesforce

Description: Implemented a comprehensive Salesforce solution for BTS Motors, an automobile dealership, to centralize and automate their fragmented sales and support processes. The goal was to provide an efficient and seamless experience under a single cloud-based application.

Challenges & Resolutions:

  • Fragmented Systems: Consolidated disparate spreadsheets and manual processes into a unified Salesforce platform, eliminating data silos and improving data visibility.
  • Inefficient Case Handling: Designed and automated a robust case management system using Salesforce Service Cloud, improving service team visibility and customer satisfaction.
  • Lack of Sales Pipeline Visibility: Configured Sales Cloud to provide real-time insights into the sales pipeline, enhancing collaboration and ultimately increasing sales revenue. Implemented territory management to optimize lead distribution.

Measurable Improvements:

  • Reduced average case resolution time by 25%.
  • Improved sales pipeline visibility leading to a projected 15% increase in sales revenue.
  • Enhanced data accuracy and reduced manual data entry errors.

Salesforce Tools Used:

Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Flow Builder (for automation), Lightning App Builder, Dataloader.io (for data migration), Reports & Dashboards, Change Sets.

EduForce: Enhancing Student Success with Salesforce

Description: Developed a comprehensive and centralized Student Success Management System for EduForce using Salesforce. This project aimed to streamline student record management, course enrollments, and academic progress, providing educators and advisors a 360-degree view of each student's journey.

Challenges & Resolutions:

  • Disjointed Student Data: Centralized student records, academic progress, and enrollment information within Salesforce, creating a unified student profile.
  • Manual Administrative Tasks: Automated routine processes like enrollment tracking and assignment management using Flow Builder, freeing up educators to focus on personalized teaching.
  • Lack of Communication & Collaboration: Implemented features to foster better communication between students, educators, and academic advisors, enhancing the overall educational experience.

Measurable Improvements:

  • Reduced administrative workload by approximately 30% through automation.
  • Improved data consistency and accessibility for all stakeholders.
  • Enhanced student support by enabling timely guidance and interventions.

Salesforce Tools Used:

Salesforce Platform Custom Objects, Flow Builder (for automation), Lightning App Builder, Reports & Dashboards, User & Security Management, Dataloader.io.

BlackForce: Enhanced Customer Support Management App

Description: Developed an enhanced Customer Support Management Application for BlackForce Inc. using Salesforce Service Cloud. The application focused on efficient case management, entitlement processes, automated notifications, and comprehensive reporting to prioritize support for professional students.

Challenges & Resolutions:

  • Inefficient Case Prioritization: Implemented a Case Management System tailored to prioritize support based on subscription levels (e.g., Professional students), ensuring critical cases are addressed promptly.
  • Manual Entitlement Management: Designed and automated an Entitlement Process that assigns Service Contracts based on subscription levels, ensuring accurate service delivery and SLA compliance.
  • Delayed Notifications & Escalations: Created automated workflows for notifications and case escalations using Flow Builder and other Service Cloud tools, improving response times and customer satisfaction.

Measurable Improvements:

  • Improved SLA compliance by 20%.
  • Reduced average case resolution time for high-priority cases by 15%.
  • Enhanced customer satisfaction through automated communications and streamlined support processes.

Salesforce Tools Used:

Service Cloud, Case Management, Entitlements, Milestones, Flow Builder (for automation, notifications, escalations), Reports & Dashboards, Salesforce Inspector, Change Sets.

Canadian Cancer Society (CCS): Volunteer Management System

Description: Designed and proposed a Salesforce solution for the Canadian Cancer Society to streamline their volunteer application and screening process. The system aimed to provide volunteers with an easy way to apply and CCS staff with efficient tools for managing applications and screenings.

Challenges & Resolutions:

  • Manual Application Process: Proposed an online application form to capture volunteer information, automatically creating records in Salesforce and sending thank-you emails using automation.
  • Inefficient Staff Volunteer Assignments: Developed a system for CCS staff to assign applicants to specific Staff Volunteers, providing a clear list of assigned tasks and progress tracking.
  • Lack of Screening Process Visibility: Implemented objects and fields to track the screening process by Staff Volunteers, including capturing conversations and stages, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Measurable Improvements (Proposed):

  • Expected reduction in administrative time for volunteer application processing by up to 40%.
  • Improved efficiency in assigning and tracking volunteer screenings.
  • Enhanced volunteer experience through a streamlined application process.

Salesforce Tools Used:

Salesforce Custom Objects, Flow Builder (for email automation, task assignment), Reports & Dashboards, User & Security Management, Experience Cloud (proposed for volunteer portal), Email Templates.
